DESCRIPTION

LARCOD is a non-profit Community Based Organization (CBO) that is officially registered in Kenya with the goal of helping vulnerable children, youth, and women in the Rachuonyo, Kenya area. There are many children in this area who are vulnerable and/or orphan and many women who are widows due to the impact of poverty and diseases, particularly HIV/AIDS. HIV/AIDS has had a negative effect on both the individuals who are infected, and their family members and community. LARCOD is helping vulnerable Kenyan children achieve an education and empowering youth and women to rise above poverty and work toward a brighter future. LARCOD Activities include providing: School Fees, uniforms and school supplies, Feminine Hygiene supplies (Sanitary Towels, underwear) for girls, Health Education programs, Shelter, Clothing and Care, Access to Health Care, Psychosocial Support and Education, Nutrition and Food Security, Legal Protection and Prevention of Abuse and Exploitation, Educational Programs for Youths, Programs for Womenâ€™s Groups including Education and mobilization and Group Savings and Loan Programs
